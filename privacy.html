Privacy Policy

How Richmond Brake & Clutch handles your information. This is placeholder content for the demo and should be reviewed before going live.

1. What we collect

When you contact us through this website, book work, or leave feedback, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, vehicle details and any message you send. We only collect what we need to quote and carry out your work.

2. How we use it

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide a quote;
  • Book in and carry out work on your vehicle;
  • Contact you about your job or vehicle when needed;
  • Keep a record of work done for warranty and servicing history.

3. Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We only share details with third parties where it is necessary to complete your work (for example, ordering the correct parts) or where we are required to by law.

4. Website forms

Enquiry and feedback forms on this site are processed by a third-party form service (FormSubmit) which delivers your message to our email inbox. Information you submit is transmitted to us for the purpose of responding to you.

5. Storage & security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and to retain it only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law.

6. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you may request access to, or correction of, the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

7. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Call 03 544 1660 or email office@richmondvts.co.nz.
