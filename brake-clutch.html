What we do

Brake & clutch work, sorted properly

From a squeal at the lights to a clutch that's slipping on the hill, we handle the jobs that keep Richmond drivers safe and moving.

01

Brake Repairs

New pads, discs and rotors, calipers and lines. We fix the squeal, the shudder and the soft pedal so the car pulls up straight and sure.

02

Clutch Replacement

Slipping, juddering or heavy on the pedal? We replace clutch kits, flywheels and slave cylinders and get the bite point feeling right again.

03

Brake Fluid & Hydraulics

Fluid flushes, bleeds and master cylinder work. Old fluid kills braking — we refresh the system so the pedal stays firm and reliable.

04

Disc Machining

Warped or scored rotors machined back true on the lathe — often cheaper than replacing and a quick fix for that braking shudder.

05

WOF Inspections

Warrant of Fitness checks done while you wait. If something needs doing to pass, we'll show you and quote it before any work starts.

06

Brake & ABS Diagnostics

Warning light on? Handbrake not holding? We diagnose ABS faults, sensors and uneven wear and tell you straight what's going on.
